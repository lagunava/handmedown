# Privacy Policy

## Summary
Hand Me Down does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data to the app creator. Data you enter in the app is stored on your device and synced only through your personal iCloud account.

## Data we collect
We do not collect personal information from you on our servers. We do not run third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs that track you.  

## iCloud sync
Your data may be stored in iCloud and synced between your devices using your Apple account. Apple may process and store this data as part of providing iCloud services.

## Purchases
If you subscribe or make an in‑app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details.  

## Contact
If you contact support, we’ll only use your message to respond and provide help.

## Changes
If we change this policy, we’ll update the date on this page.
